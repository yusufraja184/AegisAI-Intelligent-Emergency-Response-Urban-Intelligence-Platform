# Class Responsibilities

## Application

Controls the entire application lifecycle.

---

## User

Base class for all users.

---

## Citizen

Creates emergency requests.

---

## Dispatcher

Assigns emergency vehicles.

---

## Emergency

Stores emergency details.

---

## Vehicle

Represents ambulance, police and fire vehicles.

---

## Hospital

Stores hospital information.

---

## Route

Represents the path between locations.

---

## Graph

Stores the city's road network.

---

## Traffic

Maintains live traffic information.

---

## DatabaseManager

Handles SQLite operations.

---

## Logger

Stores application logs.

---

## AuthenticationManager

Handles login and user authentication.

---

## ReportManager

Generates reports.

---

## PredictionEngine

Runs AI prediction models.
